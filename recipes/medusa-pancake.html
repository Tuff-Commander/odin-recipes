Medusa's Pancake

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Baking Powder
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Flavor

Procedure

  1. Seperate egg white from yolk
  2. Add sugar to egg white
  3. Mix untill it becomes foamy
  4. Add flour, butter, yolk, baking powder, milk and vanilla flavor to a separate bowl
  5. Add warm water
  6. Mix until everything becomes one
  7. Add foamy egg white gradually to flour mixture
  8. mix gently
  9. Bake on low heat
