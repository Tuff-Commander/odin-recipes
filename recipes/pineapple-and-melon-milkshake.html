Pineapple and Watermelon Milkshake

A simple drink that nourishes, it is easy to make and easier to drink.

Ingredients

  • Pineapple
  • Watermelon
  • Fresh Milk

Steps

  1. Peel pineapple and watermelon
  2. Dice both of the fruits after peeling
  3. Put the quantity you want in a blender
  4. Add fresh milk to the blender
  5. Blend till smooht
